ABOUT

Dear User,
This app was created by me, Lucas, during a short term "Flask" course at Infinity School (Fortaleza - CE, Brazil). The Spotter was design specifically to register, store and read training programs, being them simple or complex as training periodization.
Although this alpha version doesn't have all the features we all want, we'll keep our work to make this app fully functional and to deliver a pleasent user experience.
As for me, I'm Lucas, B.Sc in Physical Education and M.Sc in Physiology and Professor at the present moment. In the field of sport science, I was always doing research (academic), looking for answer and trying to solve problems for many different things regarding exercise. Coincidentally, I found many solutions for these problems in technology field and that's the reason that led me to learning a little more about sport technology.
Special thanks to NatorJunior for teaching me how to use flask.
